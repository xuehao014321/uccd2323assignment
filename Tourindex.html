Petronas Twin Towers

The Petronas Twin Towers, standing at 452 meters, are Malaysia’s most iconic structures and the world’s tallest twin towers.

The sky bridge was built to connect and stabilize the two buildings and is open to all visitors.

Suria KLCC is a top shopping mall at the base of the Petronas Twin Towers, featuring luxury brands, dining, and entertainment.

George Town

George Town in Penang is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its well-preserved colonial architecture and cultural diversity. The city’s streets are filled with historical buildings and vibrant street art.

Penang's food scene is legendary, with George Town offering a mix of Malay, Chinese, and Indian cuisines.

Visitors can explore museums, galleries, and traditional workshops that showcase the city's rich heritage.

Semporna

Semporna is a gateway to some of the world's best diving spots, including the famous Sipadan Island. The town is surrounded by crystal-clear waters and abundant marine life, attracting divers and snorkelers.

Sipadan Island, known for its rich biodiversity, is a top diving destination where can see turtles,barracudas , and sharks.

Semporna is also home to the Bajau Laut, or sea gypsies, who live in stilt houses. Nearby islands like Mabul and Kapalai offer relaxation in resorts.
